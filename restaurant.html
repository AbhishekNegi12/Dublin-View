Top Restaurants in Dublin

Image and Name Description and Cuisine

Hawksmoor

Located in a historic bank building, Hawksmoor offers some of Dublin's best steaks. Don't miss their famous beef-dripping chips!

Cuisine: Steakhouse

Pickle

Camden Street's Pickle serves authentic Indian cuisine crafted by award-winning chef Sunil Ghai. Try the Lahsooni Fish Curry!

Cuisine: Indian

Chapter One

A Michelin-starred restaurant offering a blend of Irish and French flavors. Expect exquisite dining with exceptional service.

Cuisine: Fine Dining

SOLE Seafood and Grill

Located on St William Street, SOLE is known for premium Irish seafood like West Cork mussels and fresh sea bass.

Cuisine: Seafood
